Complete job description:
Bickford’s Group is seeking a motivated, quick-thinking Junior Web Developer to join their in-house digital team in a full-time role. You will support the company’s 20+ brand websites (including Vok, 23rd Street Distillery, Beenleigh, Bickford’s) and three eCommerce businesses (Wheel&Barrow Home, Sippify, Karma Living). Based at the head office in Salisbury South, Adelaide SA, you’ll work closely with the Senior Developer and team to design and develop modern, UX-driven websites using WordPress and Shopify. The role includes front-end development, website updates, troubleshooting, and supporting eCommerce and brand managers. This is an ideal opportunity for recent graduates to gain real-world experience and mentorship.

Key responsibilities:
• Support front-end website development (HTML, CSS, JS) with a focus on clean code and user experience
• Build, customise, and maintain websites (WordPress, Shopify) ensuring responsive design and performance
• Troubleshoot and maintain brand websites
• Support Brand Managers with site maintenance and IT issues
• Assist Senior Developer with eCommerce tasks
• Monitor and optimize website operations
• Conduct cross-device and browser testing for quality and consistency
• All requirements (essential and desirable)
